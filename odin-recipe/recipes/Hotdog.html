Chicago-style Hot Dog

Description

Chicago-style hot dogs are a classic American dish, known for their unique toppings and flavors. This recipe features a steamed poppy seed bun, a juicy all-beef frankfurter, and a variety of fresh toppings.

Ingredients

  • All-beef hot dogs
  • Poppy seed buns
  • Yellow mustard
  • Sweet pickle relish
  • Diced onions
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced pickles
  • Sport peppers
  • Celery salt

Steps

  1. Steam the buns until soft.
  2. Grill or boil the hot dogs until heated through.
  3. Assemble the hot dog in the bun with mustard, relish, onions, tomatoes, pickles, and sport peppers.
  4. Sprinkle with celery salt before serving.
