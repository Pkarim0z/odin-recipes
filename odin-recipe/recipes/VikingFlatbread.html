Viking Flatbread

Description

This Viking flatbread is so flavorful. Using flours the Vikings might have used—spelt flour and rye flour—and moistening the dough with buttermilk rather than water, the f;atbreads have a bit of a sourdough note.

Ingredients

  • Spelt flour
  • Rye flour
  • Buttermilk
  • Salt
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Mix the flours and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add buttermilk and honey, then knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Divide into small balls, roll out into flatbreads.
  5. Cook on a hot skillet for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with butter or as a side to your meal.
